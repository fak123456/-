## Task
Produce the best Amazon EU main listing image of the **same product** as the references, on pure white. ## Do
- Background: solid **pure white #FFFFFF**, perfectly even edge to edge; the only mark allowed is one subtle realistic contact shadow directly under the product.
- Product centered, fills **75–85%** of frame; whole product and all units visible (a set of 4 stays 4).
- Soft studio light from upper-front; photorealistic premium catalog look.
- Remove props, watermarks, text, and background distractions from the reference.
