## Task
Generate one square lifestyle image showing **where and how this product is used in real life**, while keeping the product clearly identifiable.

## Composition — pick what fits THIS scene (vary across the batch)
- **A. Product in the scene (no inset):** product at a believable size, installed / in use / on a surface, still clearly recognizable. Preferred when the product reads well on its own.
- **B. Scene + small product inset:** use only if the product is hard to identify from the scene alone. Inset = a clean white-background hero shot, **15–25% of canvas width (never >30%)**, placed in **one corner only** (rounded rectangle, small square, or small circle; no edge-spanning banner), with a thin border or soft shadow.
- **C. Hybrid:** product in scene + a subtle small inset if needed.
- If several scene images use insets, vary their shape / position / size so the gallery isn't templated.

## Background per index (each a different physical environment)

## Style
- Photorealistic environment, natural lighting for the scene; product color must match references everywhere it appears.
- No marketing text, price tags, badges, callouts, or arrows.
- Recognizable faces must not be shown clearly (hands / silhouettes OK).

## Negative
